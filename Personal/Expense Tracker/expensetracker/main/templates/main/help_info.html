Expense Tracker

Welcome to Expense Tracker

Expense Tracker is a user-friendly web application designed to help you manage your daily expenses effectively. With Expense Tracker, you can track your spending, categorize expenses, and visualize your financial data for better budgeting.

Key features of Expense Tracker include:

  • User Registration: Create an account to start tracking your expenses.
  • Expense Logging: Log your daily expenses by providing information such as date, place, purchase, cost, type, and description.
  • Data Categorization: Expense Tracker categorizes your expenses based on type for easy analysis.
  • Graphical Representation: Visualize your spending habits with interactive graphs, allowing you to understand where your money is going and plan your budget accordingly.

Start managing your finances efficiently today with Expense Tracker!
